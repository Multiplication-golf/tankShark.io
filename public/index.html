How to Play

  • Welcome to TankShark.IO! Start by choosing a username and clicking "Play." Use your mouse to aim and steer your tank. Left-click to shoot, right-click to interact with objects. Your goal: survive, earn points, and dominate the battlefield. Watch your health and score to track progress.
  • Movement is everything. Use arrow keys or WASD to move, and the mouse to aim your cannon independently. This split control lets you shoot while dodging. Master it to outmaneuver enemies and stay alive.
  • As you roam the map, you‚Äôll face enemies, bosses, and chances to earn points. Spend points to upgrade your tank‚Äîboost speed, health, damage, or reload time. Choose wisely; upgrades are key to survival.

  • Customize your tank and settings. Pick a skin, toggle dark mode, hide player names, or adjust chat visibility. Set things up to match your play style.
  • The map is big and dangerous. Use the terrain to dodge fire and set traps. Keep moving to stay alive and look for every chance to earn points and upgrade your tank.
  • Whether you fight solo or team up, TankShark.IO is packed with action and competition. Stay sharp, adapt fast, and aim for the top. Good luck out there!
